Andromeda Galaxy

The Andromeda Galaxy is the closest spiral galaxy to the Milky Way and will collide with it in about 4 billion years.

Whirlpool Galaxy

A spiral galaxy with a companion galaxy, known for its dynamic structure and interaction.

Pillars of Creation

Towering columns of gas and dust in the Eagle Nebula, where new stars are being born.

Triangulum Galaxy

A smaller spiral galaxy near the Milky Way, visible in the Triangulum constellation.

Crab Nebula

The remains of a supernova explosion from 1054 AD, housing a fast-spinning neutron star.

Sombrero Galaxy

Named for its hat-like appearance, this galaxy has a bright nucleus and a thick dust ring.
